Brief

Mobile App for sharing virtual keys with the family members, friends and trusted people.

Why virtual key?

Losing physical keys has been happening for centuries since doors with locks were invented. Getting locked out of our own home is happening more often recently with the new locking mechanism in our doors. Forgetting keys is a natural phenomenon worldwide. Since most of the devices in our home are getting smarter, it is quite possible in near future that every home comes with virtual keys for doors . No worries of losing the keys, forgetting the keys and getting locked out of our own house. Sharing with others is lot simpler.
