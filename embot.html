Key Insights

According to surveys and researches, is the leading cause of disability worldwide. Depression leads to health issues, primarily heart diseases , mental issues and might eventually lead to suicide. Many don't share their depression state with others even with their friends. And current social media situation leads to loneliness and more cases of depression globally, particularly among the youth population. Also there is a shortage of mental health professionals and therapy is expensive and inconvenient.

Pain Points

  • People don't generally talk or share their mental state and mood with others especially who are introverts
  • Mental health professionals may not be available all the time
  • Therapy expensive and inconvenient regarding place and time
  • Therapist has to spend a significant amount of time to understand the core problem hence daily availability is also an issue
  • May not be there when needed the most
  • Tracking the progress is not done in the most effficient manner
  • For those who are in remote areas, getting access to the therapist is the main issue
